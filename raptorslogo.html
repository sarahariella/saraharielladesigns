Toronto Raptors 3D Logo

The Project

The goal of the project was to model, texture and render any logo of our choosing in 3DS Max. We also had to create a 10 second animation for the logo in Max as well. I decided to bring in my logo animation into After Effects after it had rendered to add some more lighting effets, etc.
