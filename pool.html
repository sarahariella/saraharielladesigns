Motion Graphics Logo Reveal

The Project

The goal of the project was to design a logo reveal promotional video for a fictional company. I chose to do a pool bar and lounge because I enjoy going to play pool with my friends. It also gave me the opportunity to portray the feeling of the lounge which I decided would be sophisticated and minimalistic, with a fun twist. The promotional video had to be 5-10 seconds long, using after effects to animate our logo reveal.
